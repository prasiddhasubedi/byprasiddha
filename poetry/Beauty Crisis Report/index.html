Beauty Crisis Report

prasiddha
your eyes
oh great, they glow,
steal every show,
like they know
they're too perfect, though.
your voice
smooth slide,
sweet tide,
I try to hide
but my brain's like
"we already died."
your hair
wow, the sway,
the perfect way,
like every breeze
just wants to obey.
must be nice
to slay all day.
your smile
not fair at all,
a curve so small
yet making
my confidence fall
one grin
and you own the hall.
your laugh
oh yes, that too,
bright, brand new,
world's upgrade
because of you.
honestly rude,
but completely true.
your whole vibe
top tier,
no fear,
stealing hearts
far and near.
I file a complaint
every year
but still
you volunteer
to stay beautiful
and interfere
with everyone's
career
of acting
like they don't care.
so yes you win again,
my friend.
beauty level: unfair trend.
and somehow
you still pretend
it's natural, which offends.
